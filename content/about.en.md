I am ZenGeekLabs. This site is where I keep notes on programming, tools, knowledge management, and the experiments worth revisiting.

Over time, it should grow into both a personal blog and a digital garden:

- write down technical notes and practical lessons
- archive useful tool experience
- keep methods that can be reused for a long time

It does not try to put a self-introduction first. I would rather let the work and the writing speak first.

If you also spend time exploring code, tools, and workflows, or you are looking for a writing system that supports long-term accumulation, there may be something here for you.
